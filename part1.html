Part 1

Selection: None



    No Selection

    Camera Movement/Rotation

  • a and d — translate view left and right along view X
  • w and s — translate view forward and backward along view Z
  • q and e — translate view up and down along view Y
  • A and D — rotate view left and right around view Y (yaw)
  • W and S — rotate view forward and backward around view X (pitch)
  • space — select objects

    With a selection

    Translation/Rotation

  • a and d — translate selection left and right along view X
  • w and s — translate selection forward and backward along view Z
  • q and e — translate selection up and down along view Y
  • A and D — rotate selection left and right around view Y
  • W and S — rotate selection forward and backward around view X
  • E and Q — rotate selection clockwise and counter-clockwise around view Z
  • left and right — select and highlight the next/previous triangle set (previous off)
  • space — deselect objects
